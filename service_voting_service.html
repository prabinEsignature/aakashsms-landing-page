What is SMS Voting? 

SMS voting is a dynamic and straightforward method of collecting votes, feedback, or opinions via text messages. Using a dedicated short code or keyword, participants can quickly cast their vote by sending a simple SMS. It’s an effective, low-cost, and scalable way to engage with a large audience, making it perfect for events, competitions, polls, and surveys. SMS voting not only simplifies the process but also ensures participation from even those with basic mobile phones

How Does SMS Voting Work?

SMS voting is simple and efficient. Here's how it works

Step one

Set Up Keywords or Shortcodes

You create a unique keyword (e.g., VOTE1, WINNER2) for each voting option or a dedicated shortcode number.

Step two

Promote the Voting Campaign

Share your voting options through various marketing channels like social media, TV, radio, or websites.

Step three

Vote via SMS

 Participants send their vote by texting the appropriate keyword or shortcode.

Step four

Track and Monitor Results

AakashSMS provides you with real-time reports and analytics to track vote counts and overall participation.
